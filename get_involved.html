COMMUNITY EMPOWERMENT

With your support ICHIO looks forward to support communities with more innovative and quality improvement health systems that will promote health, environmental protection, act as a whistle blower for tropical diseases, and also improve sanitation through it's programs. ICHIO also looks forward to find permanent solutions to Sanitation & Hygiene in slum or congested areas.

ICHIO supports reforms that ensure coverage and thereby equitable access to health services for all people. .

ICHIO carries out Service delivery reforms that ensure health care is people-centred and based on expectations and needs of people are vital for effective health promotion

ICHIO Identifyies competencies, developing curriculum and strengthening capacity for training for health promotion leadership through its internship programs

ICHIO focuses on practical linkages between Health Promotion and health systems.

Community empowerment

Community empowerment refers to the process of enabling communities to increase control over their lives. "Communities" are groups of people that may or may not be spatially connected, but who share common interests, concerns or identities. These communities could be local, national or international, with specific or broad interests.

Through Community Empowerment ICHIO addresses the social, cultural, political and economic determinants that underpin health, and seeks to build partnerships with other sectors in finding solutions.
